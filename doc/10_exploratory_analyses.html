There is significant negative correlation between Age & FTP, so as age increases, time horizons decrease. There is also a significant negative correlation between Age and Health Insurance. As age increases, health insurance coverage increases (for health_ins: 1=Yes, 2=No). There is a slight positive correlation between Age and Liquid Savings (r=.11, p=.06)

  • FTP: higher scores = longer time horizions
  • health_ins: 1=Yes, 2= No –> so lower health_ins scores means more insurance
